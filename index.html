Our Journey & History

Clorivo Home has grown from a small family business to a leading event services provider. Our journey reflects our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

2003 - The Beginning
Founded by Maria and Roberto Clorivo, our company started as a small home-based operation with a passion for creating beautiful celebrations. With just two employees and a modest inventory, we served our local community with dedication and creativity.
2007 - First Expansion
Due to growing demand, we opened our first physical storefront and expanded our product range. We invested in professional training for our team and introduced new service offerings including venue decoration and event coordination.
2012 - Regional Growth
Clorivo Home expanded to multiple locations across the region. We established partnerships with venues, caterers, and other event professionals. Our team grew to over 30 dedicated professionals committed to excellence.
2018 - Digital Transformation
We launched our online platform, making it easier for customers to browse our services and request quotes. We invested in technology to streamline operations and improve customer communication. Our digital presence expanded significantly.
2023 - Present Day
Today, Clorivo Home is recognized as an industry leader with over 15,000 successful events completed. We continue to innovate, expand our services, and maintain the personal touch that made us successful from day one.

Our Process

We've streamlined our process to make planning your event as smooth and enjoyable as possible. Here's how we work with you from start to finish.

Step 1: Initial Consultation

We start by understanding your vision, budget, and specific requirements. Our consultants ask detailed questions to ensure we capture every aspect of your celebration. This is where we build the foundation for a successful event.

Step 2: Design & Planning

Our creative team develops a detailed plan including decoration concepts, color schemes, and layout designs. We present visual mockups and discuss any adjustments until you're completely satisfied with the design.

Step 3: Preparation & Coordination

We source all materials, coordinate with other vendors, and prepare detailed setup instructions. Our team conducts site visits to ensure everything is ready for a smooth execution on the day of your event.

Step 4: Execution & Support

On event day, our professional team arrives early to set up and coordinate all decorations. We remain on-site throughout the event to ensure everything runs smoothly and make any necessary adjustments.
